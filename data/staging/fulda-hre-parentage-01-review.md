# Fulda HRE Parentage 01 Review

This packet promotes a bounded parentage span from the Princely Abbey of Fulda
to the Holy Roman Empire.

## Accepted Parentage

| Child | Parent | Span | Sources |
|---|---|---|---|
| Princely Abbey of Fulda | Holy Roman Empire | 1221..1802 | src-wikipedia-princely-abbey-fulda, src-wikidata-q12548 |

## Evidence

- The accepted Fulda source bounds the modeled child title to `1221..1802` and
  identifies the entity as a princely abbey / prince-bishopric and
  ecclesiastical principality.
- The same source records Fulda's imperial context under the Holy Roman Empire
  and says that from 1221 the abbots served as princes of the Holy Roman
  Empire.
- The accepted Holy Roman Empire source bounds the modeled parent title to
  `962..1806`.

## Boundary

- The promoted span is limited to Fulda's already accepted princely-abbey title
  span.
- No monastery foundation claim, abbey school history, offices, Imperial Diet
  voting mechanics, Upper Rhenish Circle membership, prince-bishopric elevation,
  Nassau-Orange-Fulda transfer, later French/Austrian/Prussian custody, or
  modern diocese history is promoted.
