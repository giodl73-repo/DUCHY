# Prince-Bishopric of Augsburg HRE Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Prince-Bishopric of Augsburg parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for
962-1803.

## Basis

- `title-q173863` Prince-Bishopric of Augsburg and `title-q12548` Holy Roman
  Empire already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Augsburg as a state/prince-bishopric of
  the Holy Roman Empire with a c.888-1803 span.
- The source gives Augsburg's wider c.888-1803 state/prince-bishopric span, but
  the imported parentage span starts at 962 so it remains within the accepted
  Holy Roman Empire span.
- The source is accepted for text-backed parentage context only; it does not
  authorize importing holder sequences, borders, ecclesiastical jurisdiction,
  Imperial-immediacy subclaims, or secularization details.

## Boundary

separation mechanics, Swabian Circle administration, ecclesiastical diocese
extent, Bavarian mediatization details, borders, or later diocesan continuity
claims are imported in this packet.
