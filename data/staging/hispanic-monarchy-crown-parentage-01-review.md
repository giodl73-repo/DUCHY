# Hispanic Monarchy Crown Parentage Review

## Decision

Promote Hispanic Monarchy as `title-q766543` and import Crown of Aragon and
Crown of Castile parentage under it for 1479-1700.

## Basis

- `title-q204920` Crown of Aragon and `title-q217196` Crown of Castile already
  have accepted source-backed title facts.
- Wikidata structured claims record both crowns as part of Hispanic Monarchy
  using `P361`.
- Hispanic Monarchy is promoted with DUCHY's `Empire` rank as a
  super-composite parent above `Crown`. This is a DUCHY hierarchy modeling
  rank, not a claim that the entity was formally styled as an empire.
- Parentage spans are clipped to the accepted child and parent spans:
  1479-1700.

## Boundary

This packet does not import the full Spanish succession, colonial
administration, personal-union mechanics, Portuguese or Iberian Union handling,
post-1700 Bourbon succession, borders, or component-title inventory.
