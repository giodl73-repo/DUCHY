# Duchy of Prussia Polish Crown Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Duchy of Prussia parentage under the Crown of the Kingdom of Poland for
1525-1618.

## Basis

- `title-q153091` Duchy of Prussia and `title-q171348` Crown of the Kingdom of
  Poland already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Ducal Prussia as established as a fief of
  the Crown of Poland in 1525.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted Duchy of Prussia span ending
  at the 1618 Brandenburg personal union, while remaining within the accepted
  parent title span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No Teutonic Order secularization mechanics, Prussian Homage ceremony details,
Brandenburg-Prussia personal-union governance, post-1618 Polish suzerainty,
Treaty of Wehlau or Oliva sovereignty changes, estate politics, borders, later
East Prussia administration, or Kingdom of Prussia claims are imported in this
packet.
