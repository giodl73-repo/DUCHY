# Confederation of the Rhine Westphalia Parentage Review

## Decision

Promote Confederation of the Rhine as `title-q154741` and import Kingdom of
Westphalia parentage under it for 1807-1813.

## Basis

- `title-q153943` Kingdom of Westphalia already has accepted source-backed
  title facts.
- Wikidata structured claims record Kingdom of Westphalia membership in
  Confederation of the Rhine using `P463`.
- Confederation of the Rhine is promoted with DUCHY's composite `Crown` rank as
  a confederation parent above `Kingdom`.
- The parentage span is the overlap of Westphalia's accepted 1807-1813 span and
  Confederation of the Rhine's structured 1806-1813 span.

## Boundary

This packet does not import Confederation member inventories beyond
Westphalia, Napoleonic protectorate mechanics, military obligations, borders,
constitutional acts, Confederation dissolution details, or successor claims.
