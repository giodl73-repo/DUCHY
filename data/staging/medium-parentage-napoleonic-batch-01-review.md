# Medium Parentage Napoleonic Batch 01 Review

## Decision

Import three medium-priority kingdom parentage spans under the First French
Empire:

| Child | Parent | Span |
|---|---|---:|
| Kingdom of Holland | First French Empire | 1806-1810 |
| Kingdom of Etruria | First French Empire | 1804-1807 |
| Kingdom of Italy (Napoleonic) | First French Empire | 1805-1814 |

## Basis

- Each child title and `title-q71084` First French Empire already has accepted
  source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text sources identify each child kingdom as a client state of the
  French Empire or Napoleon's French Empire.
- The imported spans are clipped to the overlap of the accepted child title
  span, accepted First French Empire span, and reviewed text source range.
- Kingdom of Etruria starts in 1801, but the imported parentage starts in 1804
  because the accepted First French Empire parent starts in 1804.

## Boundary

No Napoleonic administrative details, personal-union mechanics, annexation
events, treaty terms, borders, holders, successor departments, or post-1814
settlement claims are imported in this packet.
