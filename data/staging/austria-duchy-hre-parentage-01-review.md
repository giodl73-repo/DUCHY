# Duchy of Austria HRE Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Duchy of Austria parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for 1156-1453.

## Basis

- `title-q3624335` Duchy of Austria and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the Duchy of Austria as a medieval
  principality/state within the Holy Roman Empire, established in 1156 by the
  Privilegium Minus and ending as a duchy when the archducal title was
  recognized in 1453.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted Duchy of Austria span,
  1156-1453, and is fully contained within the accepted Holy Roman Empire span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No Margraviate of Austria continuation detail, Bavarian detachment politics,
Babenberg succession dispute, Habsburg dynastic claims, Privilegium Maius
detail, Archduchy of Austria continuation, Habsburg monarchy, Austrian Empire,
or later Austrian claims are imported in this packet.
