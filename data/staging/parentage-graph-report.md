## Interpretation

- DUCHY parentage is a temporal forest, not one timeless duchy tree.
- `title_edge_fill_percent` measures title-level parentage coverage for parentable ranks.
- `weighted_span_coverage_percent` measures how much of parentable title existence time is covered by parentage spans.
- Coverage and rank-skip counts use materialized parentage edges after partial replacement spans are split.
- `density_percent` in snapshots is active parent edges divided by active parentable titles for that year.
- `rank_skip_facts` are valid but indicate missing intermediate hierarchy such as duchy or crown layers.
