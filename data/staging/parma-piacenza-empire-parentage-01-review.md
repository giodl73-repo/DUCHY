# Duchy of Parma and Piacenza Empire Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Duchy of Parma and Piacenza parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for
1748-1801.

## Basis

- `title-q165040` Duchy of Parma and Piacenza and `title-q12548` Holy Roman
  Empire already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the Farnese duchies as placed under the
  protection of the Empire by the 1748 Treaty of Aix-la-Chapelle.
- The imported parentage span starts at that 1748 settlement and ends before the
  1801 French-control transfer, while remaining within the accepted child and
  parent title spans.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No papal creation, Farnese succession, Austrian administration, Bourbon-Parma
succession, French protectorate, Napoleonic department, Restoration after 1814,
territorial borders, title-holder, Guastalla, or Italian unification claims are
imported in this packet.
