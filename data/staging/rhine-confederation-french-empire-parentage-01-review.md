# Confederation of the Rhine French Empire Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Confederation of the Rhine parentage under the First French Empire for
1806-1813.

## Basis

- `title-q154741` Confederation of the Rhine and `title-q71084` First French
  Empire already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the Confederation of the Rhine as a
  confederation of client states of the First French Empire in 1806-1813.
- The parentage span is the overlap of the accepted Confederation of the Rhine
  span, accepted First French Empire span, and reviewed text source range.
- The source is accepted for text-backed parentage context only; it does not
  authorize importing member inventories, military obligations, borders,
  constitutional acts, or dissolution details.

## Boundary

No Confederation member inventory, Napoleonic protectorate mechanics, military
obligations, borders, constitutional acts, dissolution details, or successor
claims are imported in this packet.
