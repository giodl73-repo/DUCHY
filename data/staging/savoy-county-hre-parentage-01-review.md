# County of Savoy HRE Parentage Review

## Decision

Import County of Savoy parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for 1003-1416.

## Basis

- `title-q1232887` County of Savoy and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire already
  have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the County of Savoy as a state of the
  Holy Roman Empire with a 1003-1416 span.
- The parentage span is the overlap of the accepted County of Savoy span,
  accepted HRE span, and reviewed text source range.
- The source is accepted for text-backed parentage context only; it does not
  authorize importing count sequences, borders, Imperial-immediacy subclaims,
  or later Savoyard state transitions.
