# County of Wurttemberg HRE Parentage Review

## Decision

Import County of Wurttemberg parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for
1083-1495.

## Basis

- `title-q2991474` County of Wurttemberg and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the county as a historical territory of
  the Holy Roman Empire and as a county within the Holy Roman Empire until its
  elevation to Duchy of Wurttemberg in 1495.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted County of Wurttemberg span,
  1083-1495, and is fully contained within the accepted Holy Roman Empire span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No House of Wurttemberg genealogy, Duchy of Swabia succession, Treaty of
Nurtingen division, Treaty of Munsingen reunification, Duchy of Wurttemberg
continuation, electorate, kingdom, or post-HRE claims are imported in this
packet.
