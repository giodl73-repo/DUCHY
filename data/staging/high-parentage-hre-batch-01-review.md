# High-Priority HRE County and Duchy Parentage Batch 01 Review

## Decision

Import four high-priority parentage gaps under the Holy Roman Empire:

- Duchy of Westphalia for 1180-1803.
- County of Ravensberg for 1140-1806.
- County of Montbeliard for 1042-1793.
- County of Namur for 981-1795.

## Basis

- All four child titles and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire already have
  accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text sources identify each child as a state, county, or duchy of
  the Holy Roman Empire.
- Ravensberg parentage is bounded at 1806 because the accepted child title
  extends one year beyond the accepted Holy Roman Empire span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank for each fact under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No predecessor polities, dynastic genealogies, administrative-circle details,
personal unions, French Revolutionary occupation detail, post-HRE successor
settlements, modern regional claims, or province boundaries are imported in this
batch.
