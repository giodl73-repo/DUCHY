# Margraviate of Austria Holy Roman Empire Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Margraviate of Austria parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for
976-1156.

## Basis

- `title-q283627` Margraviate of Austria and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the Margraviate of Austria as a
  southeastern frontier march of the Holy Roman Empire from 976 until its
  elevation to the Duchy of Austria in 1156.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted Margraviate of Austria span,
  976-1156, while remaining within the accepted Holy Roman Empire span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No Frankish frontier structures, Bavarian ducal subordination, Babenberg
genealogy, Hungarian border administration, Duchy of Austria continuation,
Privilegium Minus detail, March of Pannonia, Hungarian March, or later Austrian
state claims are imported in this packet.
