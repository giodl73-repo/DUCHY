# Kingdom of Thessalonica Latin Empire Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Kingdom of Thessalonica parentage under the Latin Empire for 1204-1224.

## Basis

- `title-q325461` Kingdom of Thessalonica and `title-q178897` Latin Empire
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the Kingdom of Thessalonica as a
  short-lived Crusader state established as a vassal of the Latin Empire and
  ending with its fall to Epirus in 1224.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted Kingdom of Thessalonica span,
  1204-1224, and is fully contained within the accepted Latin Empire span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No Fourth Crusade narrative, Byzantine predecessor, Boniface succession,
Lombard rebellion, Epirus conquest detail, titular claimants, or post-1224
successor claims are imported in this packet.
