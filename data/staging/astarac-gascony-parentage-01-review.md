# County of Astarac Duchy of Gascony Parentage Review

## Decision

Promote Duchy of Gascony as an accepted Duchy title and import County of
Astarac parentage under it for 920-1300.

## Basis

- `title-q1541613` County of Astarac already has accepted source-backed title
  facts.
- Duchy of Gascony is promoted as `title-q2295939` with minimal accepted
  source-backed name, rank, and existence facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Astarac as a county formed out of the
  partition of the Duchy of Gascony.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted County of Astarac span,
  920-1300, while remaining within the accepted parent title span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No later province, House of Astarac genealogy, Armagnac relationship, Gascon
stronghold mapping, Gascony-Aquitaine succession, English Gascony, borders,
title-holder sequence, or post-medieval administrative claims are imported in
this packet.
