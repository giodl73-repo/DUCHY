# Kalmar Union Sweden Parentage Review

## Decision

Correct Kalmar Union from `Kingdom` to DUCHY's composite `Crown` rank and
import Sweden parentage under Kalmar Union for 1397-1523.

## Basis

- `title-q34` Sweden and `title-q62623` Kalmar Union already have accepted
  source-backed title facts.
- Wikidata structured claims record Sweden membership in Kalmar Union using
  `P463`.
- Kalmar Union is a personal union of kingdoms, so DUCHY models it as a
  composite `Crown` parent above `Kingdom`, matching the existing rank policy
  used for composite crowns and commonwealths.
- The parentage span is the overlap of Sweden's open accepted span and Kalmar
  Union's accepted 1397-1523 span.

## Boundary

This packet imports only the Sweden -> Kalmar Union bridge. It does not import
Denmark or Norway under the union, Danish royal holders, union-break mechanics,
Scandinavian borders, or post-union successor claims.
