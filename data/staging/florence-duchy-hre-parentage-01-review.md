# Duchy of Florence Holy Roman Empire Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Duchy of Florence parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for 1532-1569.

## Basis

- `title-q2252973` Duchy of Florence and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed Duchy of Florence text source ties the duchy's creation to
  Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor, and states that the emperor determined
  Florence's government.
- The already accepted Grand Duchy of Tuscany text source supplies the
  supporting imperial-fief context for Florence.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted Duchy of Florence span,
  1532-1569, while remaining within the accepted Holy Roman Empire span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No Medici genealogy, republican constitution, siege operations, Siena conquest,
Elba purchase, naval base, succession dispute, Grand Duchy of Tuscany title
continuation, papal-imperial title dispute, or later Tuscany-Habsburg claims are
imported in this packet.
