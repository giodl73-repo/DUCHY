# Grand Duchy of Berg Rhine Confederation Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Grand Duchy of Berg parentage under the Confederation of the Rhine for
1806-1813.

## Basis

- `title-q249428` Grand Duchy of Berg and `title-q154741` Confederation of the
  Rhine already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Berg's 1806 reorganization as the Grand
  Duchy of Berg and its short existence ending with Napoleon's defeat in 1813.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted Grand Duchy of Berg span,
  1806-1813, and is fully contained within the accepted Confederation of the
  Rhine span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No earlier County or Duchy of Berg lineage, Jülich-Cleves-Berg union,
Wittelsbach rule, French annexation detail, rulers, Napoleonic administration
detail, or post-1813 Prussian settlement is imported in this packet.
