# Prince-Bishopric of Trent HRE Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Prince-Bishopric of Trent parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for
1027-1803.

## Basis

- `title-q1231403` Prince-Bishopric of Trent and `title-q12548` Holy Roman
  Empire already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Trent as an Imperial estate/state of the
  Holy Roman Empire with a 1027-1803 span.
- The parentage span is the overlap of the accepted Trent span, accepted HRE
  span, and reviewed text source range.
- The source is accepted for text-backed parentage context only; it does not
  authorize importing narrative events, holders, borders, or administrative
  subdivisions.

## Boundary

No Trentino border geometry, prince-bishop holder sequence, Tyrol succession,
Napoleonic secularization detail, Austrian crown-land transition, or later
Italian territorial claims are imported in this packet.
