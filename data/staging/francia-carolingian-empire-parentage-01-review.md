# Francia Carolingian Empire Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Francia parentage under the Carolingian Empire for 800-843.

## Basis

- `title-q146246` Francia and `title-q31929` Carolingian Empire already have
  accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the Carolingian Empire as a Frankish
  empire starting with Charlemagne's imperial coronation in 800 and divided
  after the Treaty of Verdun in 843.
- The imported parentage span is the overlap of the reviewed Carolingian Empire
  start with the accepted Francia span ending in 843.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No Carolingian holder sequence, imperial coronation theology, Treaty of Verdun
partition details, West/Middle/East Francia successor facts, borders, claims to
Holy Roman Empire continuity, or feudalism/legacy claims are imported in this
packet.
