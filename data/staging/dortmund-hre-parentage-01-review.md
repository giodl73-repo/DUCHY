# Dortmund HRE Parentage 01 Review

This packet promotes a bounded parentage span from the Free imperial city of
Dortmund to the Holy Roman Empire.

## Accepted Parentage

| Child | Parent | Span | Sources |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free imperial city of Dortmund | Holy Roman Empire | 1220..1803 | src-britannica-dortmund, src-britannica-imperial-city-hre, src-wikidata-q12548 |

## Evidence

- The accepted Dortmund source says Dortmund became a free imperial city in
  1220 and lost its imperial rights in 1803.
- The accepted Holy Roman Empire source bounds the modeled parent title to
  `962..1806`.
- The Britannica imperial city source defines imperial cities as cities and
  towns of the Holy Roman Empire subject only to imperial or German royal
  authority.

## Boundary

- The promoted span is limited to Dortmund's already accepted free imperial city
  title span.
- No city government, Hanseatic status, trade history, urban rights, diet vote,
  constitutional mechanics, territorial extent, population, Grand Duchy of Berg,
  Prussian administration, or modern municipal history is promoted.
