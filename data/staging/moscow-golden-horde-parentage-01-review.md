# Grand Principality of Moscow Golden Horde Parentage Review

## Decision

Promote Golden Horde as an accepted Empire title and import Grand Principality
of Moscow parentage under it for 1263-1478.

## Basis

- `title-q170770` Grand Principality of Moscow already has accepted
  source-backed title facts.
- Golden Horde is promoted as `title-q141472` with minimal accepted
  source-backed name, rank, and existence facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Golden Horde suzerainty over Moscow and
  identifies the 1480 end of Tatar suzerainty.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted Moscow title span,
  1263-1478, while remaining within the accepted parent title span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No tribute mechanics, grand-princely patent competition, Horde internal
succession, Moscow-Tver rivalry, Novgorod annexation, Russian centralized-state
formation, 1480 military campaign details, later Great Horde claims, or Tsardom
of Russia claims are imported in this packet.
