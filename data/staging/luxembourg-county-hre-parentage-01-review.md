# County of Luxembourg HRE Parentage Review

## Decision

Import County of Luxembourg parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for 963-1354.

## Basis

- `title-q5177890` County of Luxembourg and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the County of Luxembourg as a state of
  the Holy Roman Empire from 963 until elevation to the Duchy of Luxembourg in
  the mid-14th century.
- The imported parentage span follows the accepted County of Luxembourg span,
  963-1354, and is fully contained within the accepted Holy Roman Empire span.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No Ardennes genealogy, Luxembourg dynasty, imperial elections, Duchy of
Luxembourg continuation, Burgundian or Habsburg Netherlands, French annexation,
grand-duchy, or later Luxembourg claims are imported in this packet.
