## Evidence

- The accepted Kalmar Union source identifies the union as a personal union of
  the kingdoms of Denmark, Sweden, and Norway from `1397..1523`.
- Denmark's accepted title span is open from `800..`, so the full Kalmar Union
  span is within the accepted child and parent spans.
- Medieval Norway's accepted title span is `872..1397`, so only the `1397`
  endpoint overlaps the accepted Kalmar Union span.
- Sweden has already been imported under the Kalmar Union using the same
  composite `Crown` rank policy.

## Boundary

- This packet imports only the Denmark and medieval Norway parentage bridge
  facts.
- No Danish royal holders, Swedish union-break mechanics, Norwegian succession,
  Scandinavian borders, Denmark-Norway successor claims, post-1523 history, or
  modern Denmark/Norway continuity claims are promoted.
