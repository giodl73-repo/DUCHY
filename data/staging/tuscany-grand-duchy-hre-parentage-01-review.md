# Grand Duchy of Tuscany HRE Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Grand Duchy of Tuscany parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for
1575-1801.

## Basis

- `title-q154849` Grand Duchy of Tuscany and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source states that Florence was claimed as an Imperial fief
  and that Maximilian II confirmed the grand-ducal elevation by Imperial diploma
  in 1575.
- The imported parentage span starts at the imperial confirmation and ends with
  the first Grand Duchy period's dissolution into the Kingdom of Etruria in
  1801, while remaining within the accepted child and parent title spans.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No papal-bull validity dispute, Medici succession, Spanish claims,
Habsburg-Lorraine inheritance, secundogeniture, Napoleonic client-state,
restoration after 1814, Italian unification, borders, title-holder, or later
Tuscany claims are imported in this packet.
