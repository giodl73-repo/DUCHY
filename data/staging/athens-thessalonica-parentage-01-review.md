# Duchy of Athens Thessalonica Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Duchy of Athens parentage under the Kingdom of Thessalonica for
1205-1224.

## Basis

- `title-q334714` Duchy of Athens and `title-q325461` Kingdom of Thessalonica
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Athens as originally a vassal state of
  the Kingdom of Thessalonica until Thessalonica was captured in 1224.
- The imported parentage span starts with the accepted Duchy of Athens span and
  ends with the accepted Kingdom of Thessalonica end year.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No Fourth Crusade narrative, Byzantine predecessor, Achaea suzerainty dispute,
Sicilian or Aragonese suzerainty, Venetian control, Morea tributary status,
Ottoman conquest, titular claims, or post-1224 parentage is imported in this
packet.
