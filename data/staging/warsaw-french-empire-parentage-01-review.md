# Duchy of Warsaw French Empire Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Duchy of Warsaw parentage under the First French Empire for 1807-1815.

## Basis

- `title-q152115` Duchy of Warsaw and `title-q71084` First French Empire already
  have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies the Duchy of Warsaw as a satellite/client
  state of the French Empire with an 1807-1815 span.
- The parentage span is the overlap of the accepted Duchy of Warsaw span,
  accepted First French Empire span, and reviewed text source range.
- The source is accepted for text-backed parentage context only; it does not
  authorize importing treaty terms, military administration, borders, holders,
  or Polish succession claims.

## Boundary

No Treaties of Tilsit detail, personal union with Saxony, military control,
departmental administration, territorial expansion in 1809, Congress of Vienna
partition, borders, or successor-title mechanics are imported in this packet.
