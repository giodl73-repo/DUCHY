# Kingdom of Naples Crown of Aragon Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Kingdom of Naples parentage under the Crown of Aragon for 1442-1458.

## Basis

- `title-q173065` Kingdom of Naples and `title-q204920` Crown of Aragon already
  have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Naples as part of the Crown of Aragon in
  1442-1458 after Alfonso V's conquest.
- The imported parentage span starts at the Aragonese conquest and ends at the
  post-Alfonso separation under Ferdinand I, while remaining within the accepted
  child and parent title spans.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.
