# Benevento Lombards Parentage 01 Review

This packet promotes a bounded parentage span from the Duchy of Benevento to
the Kingdom of the Lombards.

## Accepted Parentage

| Child | Parent | Span | Sources |
|---|---|---|---|
| Duchy of Benevento | Kingdom of the Lombards | 577..774 | src-wikipedia-duchy-benevento, src-wikidata-q854415 |

## Evidence

- The accepted Duchy of Benevento source identifies the duchy as a vassal state
  of the Kingdom of the Lombards and bounds the duchy to `577..774`.
- The accepted Kingdom of the Lombards source bounds the parent title to
  `568..774`.

## Boundary

- This packet promotes only the duchy's vassalage to the Kingdom of the
  Lombards during the accepted duchy span.
- No Principality of Benevento continuation, Frankish suzerainty, Byzantine
  predecessor, rump-state independence, rulers, territorial expansion, or
  southern Lombard continuity claims are promoted.
