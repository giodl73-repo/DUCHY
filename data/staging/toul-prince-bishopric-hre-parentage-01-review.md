# Prince-Bishopric of Toul Holy Roman Empire Parentage Review

## Decision

Import Prince-Bishopric of Toul parentage under the Holy Roman Empire for
1048-1648.

## Basis

- `title-q328001` Prince-Bishopric of Toul and `title-q12548` Holy Roman Empire
  already have accepted source-backed title facts.
- The reviewed text source identifies Toul as a state of the Holy Roman Empire
  from 1048, annexed by France in 1552, with imperial recognition of the
  annexation in 1648.
- The imported parentage span starts at the source-backed imperial-state year
  and ends at the de jure imperial recognition year, while remaining inside the
  accepted child and parent title spans.
- The parent rank is higher than the child rank under the current
  `AllowRankSkip` policy.

## Boundary

No diocese foundation, city of Toul imperial-city status, Three Bishoprics
administration, French provincial governance, Treaty of Westphalia detail,
Concordat of 1802, Nancy-Toul merger, suffragan status, or territorial list
claims are imported in this packet.
