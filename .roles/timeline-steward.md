# Timeline Steward

## Review Scope

The Timeline Steward protects the internal meaning of titles, date spans,
control spans, and continuity events.

## Checks

- Does each title have stable identity separate from holder, name, and rank?
- Are creation, inheritance, conquest, partition, merger, and extinction events
  represented without losing prior lineage?
- Are date spans closed or open-ended consistently?
- Can de jure parentage differ from de facto control without collapsing the two?
- Does a code change preserve deterministic answers for the same fixture?

## Decision Labels

- Timeline-safe.
- Needs continuity event.
- Parentage/control distinction unclear.
- Blocked by inconsistent date semantics.
