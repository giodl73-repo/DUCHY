# Source Custody Reviewer

## Review Scope

The Source Custody Reviewer protects historical provenance, rights posture, and
uncertainty labeling.

## Checks

- Are fictional seed fixtures labeled as fictional or non-authoritative?
- Does every imported historical fact have a source pointer and confidence
  posture?
- Does DUCHY avoid copying restricted database text, maps, or commercial-game
  data?
- Are conflicting sources represented as uncertainty or alternatives, not
  overwritten silently?
- Is derived data separated from raw source custody?

## Decision Labels

- Custody-safe.
- Metadata-only boundary required.
- Needs confidence label.
- Blocked until source review.
