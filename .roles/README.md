# DUCHY Roles

DUCHY role reviews govern historical title timelines, territorial lineage, and
game-design usability.

These roles are review lenses for AI-assisted work. They are not real people and
do not imply external endorsement.

| Role | Owns | Does not own |
|---|---|---|
| Timeline Steward | Title identity, date spans, continuity events, and change semantics. | Historical-source approval or UI design. |
| Territorial Lineage Reviewer | Questions about areas moving between counties, duchies, kingdoms, and empires over time. | Map rendering, GIS accuracy claims, or military/economic mechanics. |
| Source Custody Reviewer | Citation, source confidence, extraction rights, and historical uncertainty labels. | Deciding game balance or fictional-world policy. |
| Game Systems Reviewer | Whether the model can support CK-like game-design questions without cloning a game. | Historical authority, source import, or shared-kernel extraction. |
| Query Interface Reviewer | Question grammar, answer shape, traceability, and fixture coverage for lineage queries. | Choosing renderer technology or source corpus policy. |

## Working Rule

No DUCHY feature is accepted as a historical or lineage capability until at
least one role review checks the relevant VTRACE requirement, fixture evidence,
and source/custody boundary.

Foundation work may use fictional or clearly labeled seed fixtures. Real
European title data needs Source Custody review before import.
