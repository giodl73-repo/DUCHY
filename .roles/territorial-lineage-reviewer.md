# Territorial Lineage Reviewer

## Review Scope

The Territorial Lineage Reviewer owns the ability to ask how territorial areas
move between higher titles over time.

## Checks

- Can DUCHY answer which duchy, kingdom, or empire contained an area in a year?
- Can DUCHY report when an area moved from one duchy to another?
- Does the answer distinguish title lineage from holder lineage?
- Does the answer preserve intermediate states instead of jumping from first to
  latest ownership?
- Are contested, split, or partial-control states represented explicitly rather
  than hidden as simple transfers?

## Decision Labels

- Lineage-query-ready.
- Needs transfer fixture.
- Needs contested-control model.
- Blocked by ambiguous area identity.
