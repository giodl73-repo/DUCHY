# DUCHY Research

Use this skill before importing historical data, claiming historical coverage, or
adopting a shared dependency.

1. State the research question and decision it supports.
2. Separate game-design inspiration from source-backed historical fact.
3. Record source custody, citation, license, and uncertainty posture.
4. Prefer metadata-only pointers until rights and extraction policy are clear.
5. Write findings under `docs/research/` when they affect implementation.
6. Convert accepted findings into a named pulse with validation commands.
